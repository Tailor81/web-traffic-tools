About the Web Traffic Analyzer

The AI-Solutions Web Traffic Analyzer is a powerful tool designed to help businesses understand their web traffic patterns and visitor behavior through advanced data analysis.

Features

  • Log Analysis

    Upload and analyze IIS web server logs with ease

  • Interactive Dashboards

    Visualize traffic patterns with customizable views

  • Geographic Tracking

    Monitor visitor distribution worldwide

  • Conversion Analysis

    Track page performance and conversion rates

  • Custom Reports

    Generate and share professional reports

  • Data Export

    Export your analysis in various formats

How It Works

  1. Upload your IIS web server logs
    Our system accepts standard IIS log formats and processes them securely.
  2. Data processing and enrichment
    We parse, clean, and enrich your log data with geographic information and page categorization.
  3. Interactive dashboards
    Explore your data through intuitive and interactive visualizations tailored to your needs.
  4. Generate reports
    Create customized reports to share insights with your team and stakeholders.
